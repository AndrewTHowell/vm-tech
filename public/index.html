About Us

We are Van Mildert College's Tech committee, we facilitate any tech needs the JCR has and keeps its inventory modern and organised

Vast Inventory

We have access to a large stockpile of tech, meaning whatever event you host, we will be prepared and will amaze your audience

Knowledge and Experience

Most of our team have worked in Sound&Light since before University, we collectively have decades of experience working many events in many venues

Friendly Faces

We pride ourselves on our customer relations, and aim to keep you smiling before, during and after your event

Services

We offer tech to both Van Mildert events and External Events, offering a wide array of equipment and any technical skills you need

Lighting

We can light multiple event types: on-stage performances, solo acts, and BOPs. All with colour and animation.

Sound

We can sound op any size event, from a poetry recital to an on-stage ensemble, with Voice, Instrument and Wireless mics

Control

All our Sound&Light equipment is coordinated from our modern mixing desks, giving us full control over RGB lighting animations and sound levels
